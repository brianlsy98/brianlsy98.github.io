About Me

Hi all! I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical Computer Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin, working with Prof. David Z. Pan. I received my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) from Seoul National University with highest honor, in 2023.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in Graph Machine Learning, focusing on Graph Representation Learning and Graph Generative Models. I believe that graph is one of the most fundamental way to view data in various domain, and permutation invariance/equivariant machine learning techniques will lead to efficient training of AI models.

  • Graph Representation Learning:
    • Pretraining Graph Neural Networks for Device-level Circuits using Graph Contrastive Learning
  • Graph Generative Models:
    • Searching for new topics!
  • Others:
    • AMS Circuit Generation using Large Language Models [AAAI'25]
    • Transistor Placement Optimization using Simulated Annealing [DATE'24]
    • Delay Locked-Loop Circuit Design with 28nm CMOS Technology [ITC-CSCC'22]
